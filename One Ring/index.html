Hour of Code
The Hour of code is a project designed to teach student of any age a programming language using blocks, usually taught for children of early ages for them to understand how to think like a programmer which will come in a use when they need to solve problem. During November 18th, 2022, Muaataz A. Ismaeel, Rashad Hummatov, Farhad Aliyev and Murad Mammadov have went on to the school “British School in Baku” and have taught the 7th graders how the hour of code work and worked with them to solve problems and for their corporation with us, we have decided to award them with an official certificate with the help of the 3 organizations, Hour Of Code, ADA University and British School in Baku. We deeply thank them for their help in making our objective possible.

Our Name and Logo
One Ring
While selecting this name we were inspired by a novel, The Lord of the Rings written by J.R.R. Tolkien. In this book there are lots of powerful rings, but only "One Ring" can control all of them and it is the most powerful ring. So there are lots of great teams but, the most creative and simple is ours. Why simple? Our Website, Name and Logo are simple, because great powers don't need to tell something about their powers. "One Ring" is simple, but it implies the greatest of authority. Due to this, we thought simple = power, and made everything simple.
