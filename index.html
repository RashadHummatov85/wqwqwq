Code Hour

Hello everyone,I want to announce that me and my colleagues (Muaataz Abdulhakeem Ismaeel, Murad Mammadov, and, Farhad Aliyev) conducted the #HourOfCode event at the British School in Baku. The main purposes of the hour of code are to introduce coding algorithms and arouse curiosity about coding in children’s minds. We have organized this event with 7th grade children. The event was held on November 18th at 2pm. Before I began talking about Code.org and practicing answering questions, we provided some background information on ADA University. After this, we gave general information about Code.org and explained the logic of each question in the "Classic Maze" game. During the session and also at the end of the class, we answered students’ questions. We gave certificates to each of the children for their contributions. We express our deep gratitude to the ADA University, especially professor Araz and the staff of the British School in Baku, who gave us such a wonderful experience.
